About Us

Delivering the freshest catch from ocean to your table, we're committed to quality, sustainability, and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by James Richardson, a third-generation fisherman, Ocean Fresh Direct was born from a simple yet powerful idea: everyone deserves access to restaurant-quality seafood at home.

What started as a small local delivery service in Boston has grown into a trusted name in fresh seafood delivery across New England, serving over 50,000 satisfied customers annually.

We work directly with local fishing fleets and sustainable aquaculture farms to ensure that every piece of seafood meets our exacting standards for quality and sustainability.

Our Vision & Mission

Committed to revolutionizing how people access and enjoy fresh seafood

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in seafood delivery, making premium quality fish accessible to everyone while promoting sustainable fishing practices and ocean conservation.

Our Mission

  • Support local fishing communities and sustainable practices

  • Maintain the highest standards of quality and food safety
